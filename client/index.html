Why Passage Master?

For the majority of people, memorization is something that takes a lot of work. However, once they finish memorizing something, they find that they've forgotten it only a few months later. To solve this problem, we decided to create an online app that would help anyone memorize anything. With Passage Master, you can memorize, and not forget

  • Passages from the Bible, Quran, Book of Mormon, or other holy texts
  • Poems, hymns, or songs
  • Passages from books you love
  • Literally anything else you can copy and paste
Once you add your passages to Passage Master, they'll be part of a permanent list of passages, and added into the automatic review cycle. You'll review each passage at least once a month, depending on how well you know it. And you'll only need to spend about 5-10 minutes each day!
