Why Passage Master?

For the majority of people, memorization is something that takes a lot of work. However, once they finish memorizing something, they find that they've forgotten it only a few months later. To solve this problem, we decided to create an online app that would help anyone memorize anything. With Passage Master, you can memorize, and not forget

  • Passages from the Bible, Quaran, Torah, Vedas, or other holy texts
  • Poems, hymns, or songs
  • Passages from books you love
  • Literally anything else you can copy and paste
Once you add your passages to Passage Master, they'll be part of a permanent list of passages that you can cherish for a lifetime.

Features

Add Passages

You can add passages from anywhere of your choice. Just copy and paste!

Practice

Play games to help you master your passages.

Review

Once you've memorized your passage, Passage Master will remind you to review it periodically.
